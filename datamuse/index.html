Brief Music Breakdown

We've consolidated the entire day's sales at a couple grocery stores into two minutes. Each transaction within that 5-minute window gets grouped together.

Instrumentation depends on the type of purchase each customer made:

  • Gas purchases are a low rumble, like an engine passing by
  • Non-alcoholic drinks are flutes
  • Alcoholic drinks are bassoon
  • Vapes & cigarettes are trombones
  • Snacks are the piano
  • Lottery tickets are the glockenspiel
  • Other items (cashing checks, electronics) are a harp

Other elements of the sound:

Volume comes from the size of the purchase
We standardized each category, as snacks purchases are consistently smaller than, say, filling a gas tank.

Tone is building on this melody: CGEGEDC
